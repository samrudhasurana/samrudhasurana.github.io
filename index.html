PhD Candidate in Economics at George Mason University

I'm a second-year PhD student in Economics at George Mason University, and a Graduate Fellow with the F. A. Hayek Program in Philosophy, Politics, and Economics at the Mercatus Center.

My research interests revolve around the political economy of religion, and how people can find non-state solutions to deal with conflict.

How can we live in a peaceful and prosperous society? I want to get to this question, one samosa at a time.
